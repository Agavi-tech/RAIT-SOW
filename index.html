Who We Are

Welcome to Social Wing-RAIT: Where Passion Meets Purpose!

UDAAN

Udaan is chairtable event where we help the underprivileged children to get the education they deserve.

  • Udaan" is a fundraising marathon organised by Social Wing-RAIT and NSS-RAIT.This The funds go to underprivileged children's education. This marathon is usually organised at the end of winter, fresh for a new start.
  • 100 volunteers came together to make this marathon a beaming success with more than 6000 participants.
  • This event not only ensured awareness of importance of education but also sportsmanship.

Drop of Hope

Drop of hope is an event where we collect blood from the donors and donate it to the needy.

  • In the light of Founder’s Day of our college, we, the members of Social Wing-RAIT and NSS-RAIT, organise a blood donation camp called “Drop of Hope”. The medical practitioners and students of medicine from the DY Patil Hospital provide us with their invaluable support for this community welfare activity.
  • A total of 55 NSS volunteers, along with students and faculty, actively participated in the blood donation drive.Over 140 participants contributed to the event and we successfully collected 101 bottles of blood to aid those in need.
  • In addition to our college students and faculty, community members also contributed to this important service initiative.

Joyfest

Joyfest is an event where we celebrate the joy of giving to underprivileged children.

  • JoyFest, an event organised on28th of March,2024,by NSS-RAIT and Social Wing Committee was a heartwarming event where underprivileged children from a NGO, joined our college's techfest. They were divided into nine groups, each led by an NSS volunteers.
  • A total of 40 NSS volunteers joined their hands together for this event and around 50 children from the NGO visited our college.
  • From challenging technical games to exciting VR experiences, there was something for everyone. The children's faces lit up with delight as they immersed themselves in the festivities.

Frequently Asked Questions

Let us answer your questions

What is the Social Wing?

The Social Wing is where compassion meets action. We’re a group of dedicated individuals who believe that small efforts can create big changes. From organizing local drives to partnering with NGOs, we’re all about turning ideas into real-world impact. If you’re looking to make a difference, this is where you start.

What is NSS?

NSS, or the National Service Scheme, is a platform for students to step out of the classroom and into the real world. It’s about giving back, learning, and growing through service. Whether it’s community outreach, environmental initiatives, or skill development, NSS is where you turn your values into action.

Who can join the Social Wing Committee?

If you have a passion for service and a heart for the community, you’re welcome to join us. We encourage students from all backgrounds to contribute their time, skills, and energy to our cause.

Benefits of joining the Social Wing?

Joining the Social Wing isn’t just about volunteering; it’s about personal growth. You’ll gain hands-on experience, build leadership skills, and make lifelong connections—all while contributing to causes that matter. It’s a journey that’s as rewarding for you as it is for those you help.

How can I get involved with your initiatives?

Getting involved is simple—just reach out to us through our contact page or follow us on social media to stay updated on our latest projects and events. We’re always looking for enthusiastic volunteers ready to make a difference!
